Easy Baked Chicken Thighs



Description

These easy baked chicken thighs require minimum preparation; I love this quick recipe because I'm a college student!

Feel free to season the chicken with your favorite spices. I like to use cayenne powder and black pepper.


Ingredients


  • 4 chicken thighs

  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder

  • 4 teaspoons onion flakes

Directions


  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Step 2: Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.

  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Enjoy!
