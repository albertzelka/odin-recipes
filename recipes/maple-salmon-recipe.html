Maple Salmon Recipe


Description

This maple glazed salmon is delicious and very easy to prepare. I love maple syrup in everything and decided to use it in the marinade. My wife totally loved it; she wasn't a salmon fan until now.

Restaurant-worthy maple salmon recipe is as good as it gets! Plus, it's easy to make with simple ingredients you probably already have on hand.


Ingredients

  • Maple syrup: ¼ cup Of course, you'll need maple syrup!

  • Soy sauce: 2 Tablespoons Salty soy sauce perfectly balances the sweetness of the maple syrup.

  • Garlic: 1 clove,minced Fresh garlic adds bold flavor.

  • Seasonings: ¼ teaspoon garlic salt,⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper This maple salmon is seasoned with garlic salt and black pepper.

  • Salmon: 1 pound This recipe works for a pound of salmon, which should make about four regular-sized fillets.

Directions

  1. Step 1: Stir maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.

  2. Step 2: Cut salmon into 4 equal-sized fillets; place in a shallow glass baking dish and coat with maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning once halfway.

  3. Step 3: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  4. Step 4: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered until flesh easily flakes with a fork, about 20 minutes.

Enjoy!
